About the Project

Five Students

We're five students studying Computer Science at the University of Bath and as part of our degree, we must complete a year-long project. Thus, Shnip.it was born. We got the idea through our own needs- storing useful, small snippets of code can be a pain. Shnip.it aims to solve this problem.

Who wanted easy code storage and retrieval

Shnip.it is designed to make storing your code snippets easy. They can be categorised into boards, shared with your friends or contributed to by whomever you choose. You can rate them, comment on them, even favourite the ones you want to save for later.
